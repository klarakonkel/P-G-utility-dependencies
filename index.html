Utility framework

This explorer implements a stylized multi-level utility model from the thesis: company strategic utility combines concave profit and market-share terms with innovation speed and a variance penalty; manager, employee, and team utilities capture stakeholder incentives that may align or conflict with that strategy.

Sliders let you reproduce historical presets or test counterfactuals; charts hold other inputs fixed to show partial relationships. Alignment scores summarize tension between a company objective component and each stakeholder utility—useful for narrative, not forecasting.
